# Rulix Review Loop Script

## Direction

An 18–20 second product reel for the Rulix website. The voice should feel calm, exact, and quietly urgent—an experienced reviewer explaining why the record matters, not a startup announcer selling automation.

## Narration

Your memo can reach the right answer and still fail review.

Rulix finds the missing support inside the source.

It turns each gap into a reviewer question, keeps every response tied to the evidence, and preserves the final call.

AI surfaces the gap.

A qualified person decides.
