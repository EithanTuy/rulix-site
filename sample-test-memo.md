# ECCN Self-Classification Memo — Cryostat Unit CX-1200

**Item:** Closed-cycle cryostat platform for a university quantum-materials lab
**Owner:** Lab Operations
**Proposed Classification:** EAR99

## Item Description

The CX-1200 is a closed-cycle pulse tube refrigerator with an integrated
Joule-Thomson stage reaching 1.2 K at the sample space. It is delivered with a
digital temperature controller and control electronics for the cold-head drive.

## Performance Parameters

- Cooling Capacity: 20 mW at 1.2 K
- Hold Time: >48 hours between maintenance cycles
- Operating Pressure: 16 bar helium charge, 2.5 bar low side
- Base Temperature: 1.2 K continuous

## Use and End-Use

This system is not specifically designed for any controlled application and is
intended for fundamental research into superconducting samples. Not for use in
weapons, missile, or nuclear applications.

## Self-Classification Rationale

We conclude this item is EAR99 because it will be used in a university research
lab and the manufacturer described it as commercial equipment. No further CCL
parameter mapping was performed.
